# Low-Speed UAV Design & Manufacture

**Design & Manufacture of a Low-Speed Uninhabited Aerial Vehicle**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

A complete **conceptual to manufactured** fixed-wing UAV designed for short-duration aerial surveillance missions (precision agriculture, wildlife monitoring, and coastal patrol). 

**Key Focus**: Advanced CAD design (SOLIDWORKS), systems integration, Design for Manufacturing (DfM), additive manufacturing, and operational readiness.

- **Configuration**: High-wing pod-and-boom monoplane with V-tail and fixed tricycle landing gear
- **MTOW**: < 2 kg (CAA compliant)
- **Endurance**: 30+ minutes
- **Launch/Recovery**: Hand launch with wheeled landing

---

## 🎯 Key Highlights

- Full aircraft CAD design and detailed engineering drawings in SOLIDWORKS
- Optimized layout balancing aerodynamics, stability, manufacturability, and payload integration
- Comprehensive Design for Additive Manufacturing (DfAM) strategy using 3D printing
