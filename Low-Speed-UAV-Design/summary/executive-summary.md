# 3D Printed UAV Design & Manufacture

## Project Overview

Designed and manufactured a fully parametric fixed-wing UAV using SOLIDWORKS and FDM 3D printing.

The project focused heavily on:
- manufacturing practicality
- lightweight structural design
- modular assembly
- maintenance accessibility
- mechanical integration
- rapid prototyping

The airframe was developed as a production-oriented modular assembly with integrated structural and functional features including:
- embedded wiring channels
- removable maintenance hatches
- spar reinforcement systems
- servo integration
- battery packaging
- modular segmented fuselage sections

---
## Engineering Objectives

- Design a lightweight UAV platform under 2 kg MTOW
- Optimise the structure for FDM additive manufacturing
- Integrate electronics and control systems into CAD
- Improve structural rigidity while minimising weight
- Ensure simple assembly and maintenance access
- Develop a modular printable airframe compatible with desktop printer constraints
